Spot a Fake: Quick Guide
01
Check sender name. Real GTBank texts come from "GTBank" — never a phone number like 08012345678.
02
Look at account format. Real alerts show masked digits like **1234 — never a full account number.
03
Spot spelling errors. "Creditted", "Recieved" — Nigerian banks never misspell official SMS.
04
Verify balance math. If someone sends ₦50k but balance only increases ₦20k — it's fake.
05
Always check your banking app. VerifyNG is a first filter — your bank app is the final word.
06
Screenshots can be edited. For amounts above ₦20,000 demand to see the live banking app.
